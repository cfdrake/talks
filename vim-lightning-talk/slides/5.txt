

    Basic editing (2/2)

        %  Toggle between matching parenthesis, curly, etc.

        t<char>  Jump forwards to the next instance of <char>
        T<char>  Jump backwards to the most recent instance of <char>

        Some "verbal" commands:
        
        y$  "Yank"/copy the text from cursor until the end of line
        d$  Delete text from cursor until the end of line

        Misc:

        /<term>  Search for "term"
        n/N      Go to next or previous search term match
